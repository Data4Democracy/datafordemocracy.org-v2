#d4dhackathon

From March 31, 6PM EDT, to April 2, 2PM EDT, Data for Democracy will be holding our first ever hackathon, where groups and individuals across the world will be working together on civic-oriented data projects. This is a golden opportunity to apply your data and technology skills and interests for social good. Whether you’re new to the D4D community or have been an active member for months, and whether you’d like to jump on board with an existing project or get a new idea off the ground, you’re welcome to be a part of the hackathon!

In-person meetups will be taking place in Austin, TX, Boston, MA, Chicago, IL, Louisville, KY, New York City, NY, Seattle, WA, and Washington, DC. Other D4D volunteers around the globe will be participating remotely. You can request an invite to join us, or check out #d4dhackathon on Twitter for updates on the action.
